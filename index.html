Short discription of the charts

  1. The stacked area charts show the trend of how the amount of different types of export and import goods change from 1995 to 2019.
  2. The most imported good was services and the most exported good was minerals in last 25 years.
  3. The amount of imports and exports gross from 1995 until 2008 where the golabl financial crisis occurs, all type of import and export good dropped at that time.
  4. The advantage of stacked area chart is to enlarge the trend. Since the unit is billion. For example, the amount of imported agriculture dropped 1.5 billion in 2009 compared to 2008. If we only select agriculture, it is difficult to observe the trend, but 1.5 billion is a very large amount. However, when we select all and the chart stacked, the trend becomes obvious.
